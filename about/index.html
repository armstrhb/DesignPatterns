About

Design Patterns, an exercise in futility.. I mean gaining knowledge

Design Patterns, explained simply, was started by Henry Armstrong (that’s me) in March of 2015.

As an application support analyst at a major health insurance company in the US, I began to ache for a better understanding of better ways to accomplish common problems and tasks I encountered when creating internal utilities for my teams. I was aware of software design patterns, and the various resources online for them. I was also aware that I was slowly becoming dependent on google for an ever increasing amount of code-related things.

Hoping to stop this, I began to look more closely at these common design patterns. If I could understand these to a point that I could explain them to my five year old son (or even better my three year old youngest!), then I’d have an understanding that would stay with me, and make my resources less internet-based.
