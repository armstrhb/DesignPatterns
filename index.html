Design Patterns

Common Software Patterns, laid out and explained simply.

All developers have been at this stage at some point in their career. Some are smart, and begin to yearn for this knowledge early. Some learn these in class, not understanding their importance outside of their assignments. And some, like myself, learn these through doing things the hard way first.

Design Patterns exist for common problems found in software development. Simple knowledge of the existance of these is a start for the day we live in, when the power of the internet (read google) is at the fingertips of every developer. Having in-head knowledge of these structures, and being able to apply them directly without reference, is a big step to removing yourself from code monkey status.
